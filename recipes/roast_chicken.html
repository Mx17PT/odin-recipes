Roast Chicken


Juicy Roasted Chicken :)

Roast chicken is chicken prepared as food by roasting whether in a home kitchen, over a fire, or with a rotisserie (rotary spit).

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Butter
  • Celery

Steps:

  1. Season the chicken inside and out.
  2. Add the butter to the chicken and the pan.
  3. Stuff the cavity with celery.
  4. Bake until the chicken is fully roasted.
